A little known fact is that Shauna invented the PhotoShop that we all know and use today under the pseudonym of Thomas Knoll. After amassing a fortune with the Adobe Creative Cloud she created the rival software, Paint Shop Pro under a different pseudonym of Robert Volt. After amassing a slightly less fortune she gave up the corporate world for the freelance world. She now spends her time photoshopping Gregg T. from the Long Island Railroad onto various pictures across the MTA.
